About Us

Keymir Energy LLC FZ is a leading global procurement hub and single-source provider, headquartered in the United Arab Emirates with strategic offices in Bahrain, United States & China. As a trusted partner in the Oil & Gas, Power & Petrochemical sectors, we specialise in sourcing, purchasing, and expediting high-quality spare parts, tools, equipment, and machinery.

Quality Policy Statement

At Keymir Energy LLC FZ, we are committed to delivering high-quality spare parts and exceptional service to our customers in the Oil and Gas, Power & Petrochemical industries. Our quality policy is centred around ensuring the reliability, safety, and performance of our products.

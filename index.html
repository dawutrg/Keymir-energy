What We Do

At Keymir Energy LLC FZ, we serve as a Single-Point Source and Procurement Hub, providing top-tier products and services for industries including oil and gas, power and petrochemical, and industrial equipment. Based in the UAE, with additional offices in the USA, Bahrain, and China, we deliver reliable spare parts to support exploration, drilling, and production in the oil and gas sector. We provide essential equipment and parts for efficient power generation and petrochemical processing. Additionally, we are a trusted supplier of high-quality spare parts and equipment for various industries, helping businesses worldwide optimize their supply chains with over a decade of expertise.
